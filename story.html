Our Story

Casablanca Coffee & Grill was built for the way you eat

Healthy drive-thru food, craveable drinks, and a community-first spirit.

Welcome to Casablanca Coffee & Grill

We believe you shouldn’t have to choose between quick and wholesome. That’s why our drive-thru serves fresh, organic-leaning food, handcrafted drinks, and warm hospitality every day.

How It All Started

A group of friends with a love for coffee and clean food turned a simple drive-thru into a kitchen that could keep up with busy lives. We layered in espresso, smoothies, burritos, pizzas, and cocktails—and kept the focus on ingredient integrity.

Our Values

  • Fresh, organic ingredients when possible
  • Local farms & suppliers
  • Compostable packaging
  • Something for everyone (keto, gluten-free, vegan, etc.)

Part of the Community

We’ve served Grants Pass for years, supporting local schools, artists, and nonprofits. Whether you’re grabbing a latte at sunrise or a cocktail at midnight, we’re here with a smile.
